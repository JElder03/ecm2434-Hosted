This Privacy Policy describes how Group 4348 collects, uses, and shares personal information when you use our application Foodle.

  1. Information We Collect

    2. We collect the following types of personal information when you use our Application:
    • Email Addresses: We collect email addresses provided by users during the registration process. Emails are stored in plaintext for use in password recovery and use by our administrators. We do not consider emails to be public information, and do not display them to other base-level users.

Thus, Group 4248 do not know your plaintext password at any point, and the integrity of your passwords is maintained in the unlikely event of a database leak.

    • Password: The salted hash of your password is deleted from our database.
    You understand that upon account deletion, we keep anonymised versions of the following activity undertaken by you on our website:
    • Recipes: We store the recipes you have created even after your account has been deleted. The username attributed to your recipes is replaced with an anonymous placeholder.
